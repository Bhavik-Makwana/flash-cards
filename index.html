Welcome to JPN Tourist Flashcards

This website is designed for tourists planning to visit Japan or those interested in learning essential Japanese phrases for travel. Our flashcards cover a variety of categories to help you communicate effectively during your trip.

Whether you're a first-time visitor or returning traveler, these flashcards will help you:

  • Learn basic Japanese phrases for everyday situations
  • Navigate common tourist scenarios with confidence
  • Enhance your cultural experience by communicating with locals

Choose a category below to start practicing, and flip the cards to see translations and pronunciations. Happy learning, and enjoy your Japanese adventure!
